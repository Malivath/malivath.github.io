Welcome to Dick Heads Tobacco, where our motto is: "We take our fun seriously!" We're not just another ordinary tobacco shop; we're your one-stop destination for all things quirky and offbeat. Here, the unusual is usual!

We're a store that loves to surprise and charm our customers. The only predictable thing about us is our unpredictability. So, whether you're here to pick up your usual or try something completely new, prepare to leave with a smile and a story. Remember, life's too short for boring shopping - embrace the extraordinary at Dick Heads Tobacco!
